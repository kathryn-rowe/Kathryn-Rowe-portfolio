Sequoia ForestKeeper®
During the summers of 2009, 2010, 2011, and 2014, Sequoia ForestKeeper® ran wildlife cameras in the US Forest Service's southern Greenhorn Mountain project areas. I trained staff, organized logistics, and compiled year-end data for the 2014 field season. I also created the above map to show camera locations, data collected, and nearby fire activity that caused difficulties for the 2014 field crew to access the cameras.
As Intern Coordinator, I taught a field crew how to perform various data collection techniques, including how to estimate percent canopy cover. I created this diagram to illustrate typical canopy structure found throughout the Sequoia National Forest.
