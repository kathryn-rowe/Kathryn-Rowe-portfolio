About Me
I'm a biologist turned Software Engineer with a tendency towards environmental activism.

Prior to becoming a software engineer, I could be found tromping around the woods looking for birds and salmon. I've hooted for Northern Spotted Owls in the Shasta-Trinity National Forest, put bands on songbirds in Massachusetts, and snorkeled for salmon down the Mattole River.

I began coding during my Master's in GIS and now I don't want to stop. I love to create good things for good causes.
