Applications of Thermal Infrared Imaging
This map shows the total number and distribution of Spring Chinook (Oncorhynchus tshawytscha) and Steelhead (Oncorhynchus mykiss) observed during the Spring Chinook Dive in July 2009.
A thermal inferred flyover, performed on the same day as the dive, highlights the high water temperatures at or exceeding the salmonid thermal stress limit of 24°C.
