Melnea Cass Boulevard Project
The City of Boston retained the Charles River Watershed Assocition (CRWA) to provide guidance on stormwater management techniques for the city’s Melnea Cass Redesign Project. CRWA’s goals for the project included designing and determining the appropriate location for green infrastructure catch basins. These new stormwater inputs improve the quality of water running into local watersheds by attenuating peak rainfall events and minimizing combined sewer overflows. As CRWA’s GIS Intern, I divided the total area into five sub-sections and determined impervious and pervious surface area values. A CRWA staff hydrologist then entered the data into WinSLAMM software and analyzed the individual effects for both existing and proposed condition models.
