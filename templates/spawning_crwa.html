Available Spawning Habitat in the Charles River
Spawning analysis was reserved for the Charles River downstream of the Metropolitan Circular Dam due to fish passageway barriers. The Watertown Dam has a fish ladder used by Alewife and Blueback Herring, but as of 2012, Massachusetts Department of Fish and Game (MA DFG) captured/observed only 1 American Shad using the ladder. MA DFG stock juvenile American Shad above the Watertown Dam but do not report sightings of adults or spawning activity.
